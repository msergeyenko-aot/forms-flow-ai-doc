Permissions


Create New Roles


  1. On the Roles page (Manage > Roles) you can create or manage roles inside FormsFlow. To create new role, click "Create New Role" in the top right corner.



  2. To create new role, click "Create New Role" in the top right corner. In the popup that appears you can add a role name, role description, as well as toggle on and off what features/permissions should this role have access to.



  3. Click "Create" on the bottom of the popup when you are satified with the selected permissions.
  4. You can also edit any of the permissions by clicking the edit button beside each role in the Actions column.

Assign Roles to Users


  1. Once the role is created you can assign it to the users on the Users page (Manage > Users).



  2. Find the user you want to assign a new role to in the table and click "Add Role". In the popup click on roles you want to assign to the user, and click "Done".



    Note: If you want to remove a role from the user click on the "x" button for the role you would like to remove. The user's role will be removed immediately.
