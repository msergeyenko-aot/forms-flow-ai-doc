Templates

Premium Feature

A template is a pre-designed and structured document that serves as a foundation for creating specific categories of forms and workflow. It provides a framework with predefined fields, layouts, and formatting to streamline the process of gathering information. Templates are customizable, allowing users to adapt them to their unique needs by adding, removing, or modifying fields as necessary. These templates are particularly useful for ensuring consistency and efficiency in collecting data, as they offer a standardized format for various purposes such as surveys, feedback forms, or registration forms. Utilizing templates can save time, reduce errors, and enhance the overall user experience when interacting with the form and workflow.

You can use this feature in two ways: using pre-defined templates as a starting point or creating your own templates


Using Pre-Defined Templates

  1. Login as a designer, and go to Design > Forms. Click "New Form" in the top right corner. In the popup you can select "Use Template" to start off with a template.

  2. In the next popup you can select any template you would like as a starting point of your form. On the left side you can filter to find the template you need, either by search or by choosing a category or both. When you click on the template you wil see the full description of the template as well as the right side changes to give you a preview of the form and the flow.

  3. Once you have selected the template you like click "Select This Template" button in the bottom of the popup and proceed editing your form.



Creating Your Own Templates

  1. Start by either selecting an existing form or creating a new form by any of the methods you prefer (Build, Import, Use Template, Use AI).
  2. Then on the form edit screen click "Actions" in the top bar. This will bring up the available actions for this form. One of those actions will be "Save As Template".

  3. In the next popup select a category for the new template, as well as add a name and a description for your template.

    If you wish to update the template you have created you would need to find the form that you used to create the template and click "Save As Template" again. The details will be prefilled with your data that you can update and overwrite the template.
