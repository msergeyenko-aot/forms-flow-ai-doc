Flow-E: AI assisted form creation

Premium Feature

formsflow.ai incorporates AI chat assist support for form creation, where users engage with a GPT-powered AI chatbot. This chatbot is engineered to comprehend user queries, prompts, and instructions pertaining to form design, subsequently generating the form based on the user's input.

Steps

  1. Log in as a designer and click on "New Form" button at the top right.

  2. Click on "Use AI" in the popup that appears.

  3. Enter your form description and hit Send.



  4. The AI will generate the form and you can preview it on the right side.

  5. Now you may either regenerate, start over or continue conversing with AI asking it to modify the form that it has generated.
  6. If you are happy with the form it has created click "Select This Layout" button and continue editing the form in the form editor.
